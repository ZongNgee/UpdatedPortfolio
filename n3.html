Colour Scheme


Introduction

Tasked to use Adobe XD to create a working flight app where users can book and track their flights



Design Process

I used simplistic colours, white and light blue, as my colour template for the app. We compared to existing apps to see what kind of features should be included into the app. Users were able to book flights and select type of flight, how many people, type of payment mode, seats and the dates.
