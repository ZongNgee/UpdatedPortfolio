Software: Adobe Illustrator


Completed on 2021

Colour Scheme

Introduction

Tasked to use Adobe Illustrator to create a namecard design to display our information.Requirements inculdes our name, email, phone number and social media.


Design Process

I used simplistic colours, white and blue, as my colour template for my namecard. I used my initials for the logo and I wanted to let the colour flow into my initials which is why I chose a blue backgound for the front of the namecard. I then use white for the background of the back of the namecard as I wanted to show some contrast and did not want the namecard to have one colour.
