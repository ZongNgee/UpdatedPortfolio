Introduction

Welcome to the immersive VR horror game, "Mysteries of the Abyss," tailored for single-player thrills. Embark on a chilling journey through a haunted house, where your mission is to solve puzzles, defeat the monstrous Gloomfang, and ultimately find your escape. Brace yourself for a suspenseful experience that will test your bravery and puzzle-solving abilities. Can you navigate the sinister depths and emerge victorious from this bone-chilling abyss? The challenge awaits!



Design

During ideation stage, I was thinking of the style I want for my VR scene. I went with the horror theme as I occasionally play horror games with my friends, titles such as "Devour", "Inside the backrooms" and "Demonologist". I like the adrenaline and psychological elements that comes with it. I wanted to infuse suspense, tension and fear into my game.

- Infused forms of clues to help the player get through the game.

- Included weapons into parts of some puzzles, allowing players to wield and use.

- Each puzzle is of different difficulty so that players don't feel bored throughout the game.
